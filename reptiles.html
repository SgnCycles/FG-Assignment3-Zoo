Reptiles

At Jurassic Parse, our reptiles are some of the most fascinating residents you'll meet—ancient creatures with incredible adaptations and personalities as unique as their scales. Each reptile has its own story to tell.

Our team of dedicated keepers works every day to create environments that bring out the natural behaviors of these remarkable animals. Whether it's watching a bearded dragon soak up the sunlight, seeing a freshwater turtle glide through its pool, or learning how a snake uses its senses to understand the world, visitors get a closer look at wildlife that often goes unnoticed.

Through hands-on encounters, guided talks, and interactive learning moments, we help guests appreciate just how important reptiles are to Australia's ecosystems. They're survivors, protectors, and essential members of the natural world—and at Jurassic Parse, we're proud to share their magic with everyone who walks through our gates.

Come meet our scales, shells, and slithers up close, and discover why reptiles are some of the most extraordinary creatures on Earth!
